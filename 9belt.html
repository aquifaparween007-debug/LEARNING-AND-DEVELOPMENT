COMPRESSORS – PURPOSE AND TYPES

Increasing Gas Pressure by Reducing Volume

Definition: Compressors are mechanical devices that increase gas pressure by reducing its volume, essential for numerous industrial applications. Main Types: * Rotary Screw – Continuous compression using helical rotors * Reciprocating – Piston-driven compression for high pressures * Centrifugal – Dynamic compression for large volume applications Key Use Cases: Refrigeration systems, pneumatic tools, gas transmission pipelines, and HVAC systems. boilers.

COMPRESSORS COMPARISON

Rotary Screw vs Reciprocating


Rotary Screw: Continuous operation, less vibration, suited for large volume applications, lower maintenance. Reciprocating: Intermittent operation, achieves higher pressure, more maintenance intensive, ideal for smaller scale needs.
