Four Pillars of Steel Making

Each area represents a critical stage in the steel production process, with specialized mechanical systems and equipment requiring dedicated training programs.

MAP — Mechanical Assembly Practice

Raw Material Handling

Mechanical Assembly Practice focuses on developing hands-on skills in assembling and maintaining mechanical components. It includes understanding of tools, equipment, and techniques used in real industrial environments to ensure accuracy, safety, and efficiency.

FLP — Fluid Power

Rolling & Finishing

Covers the fundamentals of hydraulic and pneumatic systems used in industries. It includes understanding fluid flow, pressure control, and operation of components like pumps, valves, and actuators.

BBF — Basic Bench Fitting

Iron Making

Provides hands-on training in basic fitting operations such as cutting, filing, drilling, and assembling components. It helps in developing precision, accuracy, and proper use of hand tools.

LUBRICATION

Lubrication is the process of applying oil or grease between moving parts to reduce friction and wear, ensuring smooth operation.

LABELING AND ALIGNMENT

Labeling and alignment involve identifying parts correctly and positioning them properly to ensure accurate and efficient assembly.

BEARING ASSEMBELY

Bearing assembly is the process of fitting bearings into a machine correctly to support rotating parts and ensure smooth, low-friction movement.

ALIGNMENT IN MECHANICAL SYSTEM

Alignment is the process of positioning machine parts in the correct line or position so they operate smoothly and efficiently.

ALIGNMENT TECHNIQUES

Laser alignment tools provide precise, efficient shafts alihnment with digital readouts and real-time feedback.

GEARBOX BALENCING

A gearbox is a mechanical device that transmits power and changes speed or torque using gears.

BELT CONVEYOR

A belt conveyor is used to transport materials from one place to another continuously.

PUMP FUNDAMENTALS

A pump is a device used to move liquids from one location to another.

COMPRESSORS

A compressor is used to increase the pressure of air or gas.

POWER PLANT WATER SYSTEM

A power plant is a facility used to generate electricity from various energy sources.

Fluid Power

Fluid power is the use of liquids or gases under pressure to transmit and control energy in machines.

Basic Bench Fitting

Basic bench fitting is the process of cutting, shaping, and finishing metal parts manually using hand tools.
