WHAT IS BENCH FITTING?

Precision Shaping, Fitting & Assembly

Bench fitting is a fundamental metalworking skill involving the precision shaping, fitting, and assembly of metal components by hand. Using specialized tools at a workbench, technicians file, cut, and adjust parts to achieve exact tolerances and proper fit. This craft is essential in manufacturing and repair industries where machine-made parts require final adjustments, custom components must be fabricated, or damaged equipment needs restoration. Mastery of bench fitting ensures quality, accuracy, and reliability in mechanical assemblies.

ESSENTIAL TOOLS FOR BENCH FITTING

Precision Instruments & Equipment


•Files — For shaping and smoothing metal surfaces
•Calipers & Micrometers — For precise measurement
•Hacksaw — For cutting metal components
•Surface Plate — For checking flatness and accuracy

BASIC BENCH FITTING PROCESS

From Raw Material to Finished Part


•Marking Out: Measure and mark components accurately
•Cutting: Use hacksaws or shears to remove material
•Filing: Shape and smooth edges to specification
•Assembly: Check fit and make final adjustments
